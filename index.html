Entrance Exam Guide

🔬 JEE Main & Advanced

For B.Tech admissions in IITs, NITs. Syllabus: Physics, Chemistry, Maths (Class 11-12).

PCM required 2 attempts/year

🧬 NEET UG

For MBBS/BDS admissions. Syllabus: Physics, Chemistry, Biology (Class 11-12).

PCB required 720 marks

🏛️ CUET UG

Common University Entrance Test for Central Universities. Covers domain subjects + General Test.

All streams DU, BHU etc.

⚖️ CLAT

Common Law Admission Test for NLUs. Tests English, GK, Logical Reasoning, Legal Reasoning, Maths.

All streams NLUs India
